Part B – Prevention Checklist (Stay Safe Online)

-> Never enter your UPI PIN to receive money—PIN is only for sending money. -> Always read payment requests carefully before approving them. -> Do not share OTPs, UPI PIN, or bank details with anyone. -> Verify the identity of buyers/sellers before making transactions. -> Avoid clicking on unknown links or QR codes sent by strangers. -> Use only official UPI apps and keep them updated. -> Enable SMS/email alerts for all transactions. -> Immediately block your bank account if you suspect fraud.
